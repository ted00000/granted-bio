phr: PROJECT NARRATIVE Despite effective antiretroviral therapy (ART), HIV resides in infected cells as integrated proviruses and persists lifelong. The source of HIV-induced immune activation remains obscure. We will use cutting-edge, state-of-the-art single-cell multi-omic profiling methods to examine HIV-induced chronic immune activation in people living with HIV under suppressive antiretroviral therapy.
