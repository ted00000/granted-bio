title: Genetics of extreme human longevity
org_name: ALBERT EINSTEIN COLLEGE OF MEDICINE
phr: PROJECT NARRATIVE
The proposed Project 1 is relevant to public health because we will study centenarian cohorts for better
understanding of the genetics of extreme human longevity and use this knowledge to identify novel therapeutics
that slow or mitigate aging and all associated diseases. The proposed research is directly relevant to the NIA
mission in that it pertains to developing fundamental knowledge that will help reduce the burdens of human
disabilities associated with advanced age.
