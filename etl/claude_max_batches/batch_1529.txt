phr: PROJECT NARRATIVE Anopheles mosquitoes serve as vectors for the parasite causing malaria worldwide. This disease causes one of the most significant health problems worldwide, sickening 228 million people and killing over 405,000 in 2018, 2/3 of which are young children — the most vulnerable demographic. This project seeks to develop genetic technologies that would allow to genetically engineer the mosquito population, with the ultimate goal of reducing disease burden.
