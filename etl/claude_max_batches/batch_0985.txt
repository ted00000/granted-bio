phr: Narrative  We have developed a panel of cre-expressing rat lines that will allow the manipulation of different area postrema cell types that respond to endogenous and pharmacologic signals that can suppress appetite to treat individuals with obesity. These studies will allow for rational design of agents for the therapy of obesity that target the most advantageous components of these circuits for novel weight-loss therapies.
