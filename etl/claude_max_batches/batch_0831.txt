org_name: COLUMBIA UNIVERSITY HEALTH SCIENCES

Interstitial lung disease affects approximately 40-60% of adults with systemic sclerosis and is the leading
cause of death in this population. Our goal is to understand how vasculopathy contributes to the development
of interstitial lung disease in adults with systemic sclerosis. Ultimately, the results of our study will be used to
identify new treatments to prevent the development and progression of interstitial lung disease in patients with
systemic sclerosis.
