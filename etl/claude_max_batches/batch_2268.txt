title: Mutant Tubulins Confer Resistance to Pathological Tau
org_name: SEATTLE INST FOR BIOMEDICAL/CLINICAL RES
phr: Project Narrative
Abnormally folded, phosphorylated and aggregated tau accumulates in several
neurodegenerative diseases including Alzheimer's Disease. We have discovered mutations in
tubulin genes that suppress neuronal tau-induced toxicity in Caenorhabditis elegans model. The
aims within this proposal will elucidate the mechanisms underpinning mutant tubulin-mediated
