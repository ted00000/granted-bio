We propose to study the use of particles (extracellular vesicles) released by cells that produce myelin, which is damaged by the immune system in MS. Our findings in MS-like disease in mice show that these particles suppress immunity against myelin, without general suppression of the immune system, which would be advantageous over current therapies for MS.
