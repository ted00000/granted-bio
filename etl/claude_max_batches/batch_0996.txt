title: Drug Development of Skp2 PROTACs in Cancer
org_name: UNIVERSITY OF TEXAS HLTH SCIENCE CENTER
phr: Narrative
Skp2 SCF complex E3 ligase is a highly validated drug target for cancer. Due to the lack of effective inhibitors,
Skp2 SCF complex E3 ligase is considered as non-drugable. Drug development of the Skp2 proteolysis targeting
chimera (PROTAC) drugs with superior potency and safety window would transfer Skp2 to be durgable for the
treatment of cancer.
