phr: There are currently no effective treatments for tauopathies, such as Alzheimer’s Disease (AD). Uptake and spreading of pathogenic tau is recognized as a key driver of AD pathology, and is primarily mediated by tau's interaction with the cellular receptor LRP1. Molecular dissection of the tau-LRP1 interaction will enable us to devise new therapies for AD.
