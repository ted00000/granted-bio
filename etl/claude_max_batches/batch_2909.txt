Endophthalmitis, a bacterial infection inside the eye, can quickly lead to permanent vision loss. Bacteria need
iron to survive, so immune cells may overload themselves with iron to keep it away from them. This proposal
examines whether iron chelation could be an effective anti-bacterial and anti-inflammatory treatment for
endophthalmitis.
