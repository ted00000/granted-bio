degradation capabilities. This project examines the role of proteostasis in Plasmodium falciparum artemisinin
resistance and focuses on the proteasome, ubiquitination, and the unfolded protein response in Southeast Asian
and African parasites. Targeting proteostasis in P. falciparum parasites represents a new paradigm to treat
artemisinin-resistant malaria.
