title: Cell Death Regulation by Pro-Apoptotic BOK in Tauopathies
org_name: YALE UNIVERSITY
phr: PROJECT NARRATIVE
Neurodegenerative diseases, like Alzheimer's, have massive accumulation of proteins such as tau, which is
linked to the failure of the cellular recycling system known as autophagy. A unique protein called BOK connects
an intricate signaling network between autophagy and cellular survival. This project will elucidate the metabolic
pathways regulated by BOK in order to reveal potential mechanisms by which to prevent tau-mediated death of
neurons.
