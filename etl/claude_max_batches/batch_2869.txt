deformity associated with it causes substantial impact on mobility and quality of life. This study aims to address
the variability in treatment for (CMT) by characterizing bony morphology across genetic subtypes and treatment
conditions and correlating muscle morphology, bone morphology, and functional outcomes in adolescents to
better understand disease pathophysiology
