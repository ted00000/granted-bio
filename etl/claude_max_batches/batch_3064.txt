phr: PROJECT NARRATIVE The proposed project maps out the developmental trajectories of spatial hearing abilities in complex reverberant environments for school-age children with normal hearing. Findings of this work will provide intervention benchmarks to guide the fitting of bilateral hearing devices (e.g., cochlear implants and hearing aids) for children with hearing loss.
