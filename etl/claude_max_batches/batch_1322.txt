phr: NARRATIVE The ability of glioblastoma to invade brain and proliferate uncontrollably makes this tumor uniformly lethal, and this highlights the need to develop new methods to block both of these components of the malignant phenotype. In this application, we propose that non muscle myosin II represents such a target, and we will investigate how to optimize therapeutic approaches that synergize with its inhibition.
