title: Precision Medicine in Sepsis with Critical Illness
org_name: WASHINGTON UNIVERSITY
phr: Narrative
Sepsis is commonly encountered and a leading cause of in-hospital mortality. Yet, there are no specific approved
therapeutic agents for sepsis. The goal of this project is to identify biologically discrete subgroups in sepsis that
may differentially respond to therapies. Through a deeper understanding of these subgroups, we hope to improve
patient outcomes by matching appropriate therapies to individual patients.
