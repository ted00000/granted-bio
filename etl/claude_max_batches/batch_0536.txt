title: Development of a prototype clinical theranostic platform combining Magnetic Particle Imaging (MPI) and Magnetic Fluid Hyperthermia (MFH) for the treatment of brain tumors
org_name: MAGNETIC INSIGHT, INC.
phr: NARRATIVE Magnetic Particle Imaging (MPI) is an emerging imaging technology that directly quantitates magnetic nanoparticle (MNP) concentration in tissue, while Magnetic Fluid Hyperthermia (MFH) can activate those same MNPs, causing local tissue heating and killing of tumor cells. In this grant, we will advance MFH by building the first clinical-scale localized MFH system, incorporating realtime MPI tomographic temperature monitoring to enable thermal dosimetry in the target during treatment.
