title: 17α-estradiol and sex-differences in HAND with methamphetamine
org_name: UNIVERSITY OF NORTH DAKOTA
phr: Project Narrative The prevalence of HIV-associated neurocognitive disorders (HAND) remains high even with antiretroviral therapy, and there exists sex-differences in HAND; However, the underlying mechanisms remain elusive. The proposed studies are focused to determine the protective effect of 17α-estradiol, the predominant form of estrogen in brain, against brain damage resulting from combined exposure of HIV-1 viral proteins (gp120 and Tat), antiretroviral drugs, and methamphetamine.
