org_name: MEDICAL UNIVERSITY OF SOUTH CAROLINA
phr: PROJECT NARRATIVE
Studying interventions for smokers prior to cancer surgery has the potential to improve quit rates as well as
surgical outcomes in this population. We hypothesize that this pre-surgical intervention, combining smoking
cessation counseling and nicotine replacement therapy (NRT) with contingency management, will promote
high rates of smoking cessation and will be associated with better surgical outcomes.
