org_name: MASSACHUSETTS INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY
phr: Project Narrative
Our research concerns fundamental questions about the basic mechanisms responsible
for cell polarity (the development of asymmetric cell behaviors along one or more axes)
in eukaryotic cells, and how such polarity is regulated. Cell polarity enables cell
migration and directed cell growth, both of which are critical to organismal development.
Dysregulated cell migration and cell shape contribute to various aspects of disease
including metastatic malignancy.
