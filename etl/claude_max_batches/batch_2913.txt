The dissemination core publicizes the findings of Center research to a broad audience, and through this public awareness, seeks to leverage their impact toward improving health outcomes for an aging population. The existence of a core with this singular objective assures the structured dissemination of Center research in a way that would not happen without dedicated and focused attention.
