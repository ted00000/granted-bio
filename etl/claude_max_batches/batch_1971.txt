title: ADVANCING THALAMIC NEUROSTIMULATION FOR SUPPRESING ESSENTIAL VOICE TREMOR
org_name: UNIVERSITY OF FLORIDA
phr: PROJECT NARRATIVE
Essential voice tremor (EVT) affects over 40% of individuals with essential tremor (ET), one of the most common
movement disorders characterized primarily by upper limb tremor. Although thalamic deep brain stimulation
(DBS) has become the go to treatment for severe and drug-refractory ET, we are still at the early stages of
understanding its capacity to treat EVT. Here, we propose to identify thalamic targets that optimize EVT
suppression in ET patients undergoing DBS treatment
