title: From stress to anhedonia: examining inflammation and altered glutamate function as distinct or common etiological pathways
org_name: EMORY UNIVERSITY
phr: Stress is a major risk factor for mental health problems and has been strongly linked with the development of
anhedonic symptoms (i.e. “stress-induced anhedonia”), however, the mechanisms by which stress leads to
anhedonia are not fully understood. The goal of this K01 proposal is to explore the independent and additive
contributions of mPFC glutamate function and inflammatory cytokines to stress-induced anhedonia.
Elucidating these pathways will inform new targets for the treatment of anhedonia
