phr: Project Narrative For many patients with melanoma, treatment with targeted therapies initially shows great promise only for the disease to return, often without any new mutations that explain why the treatment stopped working. title: TCR Targeting Antibodies for the Treatment of T cell Cancers
org_name: JOHNS HOPKINS UNIVERSITY
phr: Project Narrative
Few treatment options are available for T cell cancers that affect ~100,000 patients worldwide each year. The
current proposal will generate a type of immunotherapy using new antibodies that specifically kill the T cell
cancers and retain enough healthy T cells to maintain a functioning immune system. This immunotherapy may
improve patient outcome and limit side effects associated with traditional chemotherapies.
