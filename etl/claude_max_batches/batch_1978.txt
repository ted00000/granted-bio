org_name: CALIFORNIA INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY
phr: PROJECT NARRATIVE
Voice and upper airway (VUA) diseases hamper a person’s speaking and breathing functions when performing
day-to-day activities. In this project, we will develop a flexible, wireless wearable device to monitor VUA
symptoms via a unique, hybrid accelerometry and electromyography sensing technology. With this new wearable
system, clinicians will be able to track patient VUA health conditions outside clinics, whereas patients will be
empowered to be more in charge of their health.
