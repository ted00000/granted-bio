title: Drosophila models of human mitochondrial diseases
org_name: HARVARD MEDICAL SCHOOL
phr: PROJECT NARRATIVE This project is to generate a reagent resource that enables the characterization and validation of Drosophila models of human mitochondrial diseases. These reagents will facilitate immunolocalization and immunoprecipitation and be used together with existing RNAi transgenic lines to validate fly models of human mitochondrial diseases. As mitochondrial diseases affect multiple organs, our proposal is relevant to most NIH institutes.
