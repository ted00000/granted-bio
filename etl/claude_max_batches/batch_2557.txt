While defective lymphatic function, as seen in lymphedema, promotes fat deposition, obesity, conversely, compromises lymphatic flow. By exploring lymphatic endothelial cell communication with other adipose tissue cell types, this proposal aims to improve our understanding of how the lymphatic system contributes to adipocyte biology and overall metabolic health.
