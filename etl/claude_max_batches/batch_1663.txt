Children with anemia, including sickle cell anemia, are at risk for not having enough oxygen for full brain
growth and development. Upon conclusion of this study, we will have a greater understanding of the
interaction of lower oxygen supply and brain development. Determining the age-dependence of vulnerability
to low oxygen and the effect on brain development will allow us to personalize treatment strategies for
children at high risk for neurodevelopmental brain injury.
