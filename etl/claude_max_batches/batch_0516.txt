title: Coordinated studies in monkeys and humans to assess the immunological impact of rectal Chlamydia trachomatis infection on protection against genital infection
org_name: UNIVERSITY OF WASHINGTON
phr: Narrative
The proposed studies will directly test for a protective effect of rectal CT on genital tract infection in a NHP
model. We will characterize the host response to rectal CT infection, compare rectal CT immune profiles in the
NHP model to those in humans, and utilize antibody functional assays to reveal how antibodies against CT
may protect from reinfection or disease. This work will determine whether rectal CT infection confers protective
immunity in primates.
