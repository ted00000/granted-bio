phr: PROJECT 3: PROJECT NARRATIVE
The proposed research is relevant to public health because it aims to develop novel therapeutic strategies for
Type I ovarian cancers including clear cell and endometrioid subtypes which are enriched with loss-of-function
ARID1A mutation resulting in reduced DNA repair efficacy and defective cell cycle checkpoint regulation. The
proposed research is relevant to the NIH mission to develop fundamental knowledge that will reduce the burden
of human illness.
