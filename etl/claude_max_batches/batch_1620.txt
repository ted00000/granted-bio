title: Defining the Translocation Mechanisms of SARS-CoV-2 nsp13 Helicase to Aid in Antiviral Development
org_name: OKLAHOMA STATE UNIVERSITY STILLWATER
phr: Project Narrative
This proposal seeks to characterize the structural mechanism by which SAS-CoV-2 nsp13 helicase
translocates along viral RNA. We hypothesize that ATP hydrolysis couples to RNA translocation through an
allosteric network. Our collaborative workflow is uniquely suited to test this hypothesis since we are able to
examine the protein from the atomic to virus length-scale. The results from this study will aid in the
development of antivirals and guide attenuated vaccine development.
