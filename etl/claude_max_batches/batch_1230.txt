diseases occur. This proposal focuses on poorly understood functions of titin in muscle, including how titin
contributes to muscle contraction. The anticipated findings likely will contribute to our understanding of the
mechanistic basis of muscle diseases, and they might ultimately contribute to the development of therapies in
patients with titin-based muscle diseases.
