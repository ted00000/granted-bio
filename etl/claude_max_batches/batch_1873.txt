title: Facilitated recruitment of MYC to chromatin by interaction with WDR5

phr: PROJECT NARRATIVE The oncoprotein transcription factor MYC is overexpressed in the majority of cancers and contributes to an estimated 100,000 deaths in the USA each year. This proposal outlines a strategy to define how MYC works with its co-factor WDR5 to drive gene expression changes that establish and maintain the malignant state. Completion of this work will expose unappreciated aspects of the molecular processes that drive cancer and how these can be therapeutically blocked in tumor cells.
