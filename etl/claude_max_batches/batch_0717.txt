research proposed in this application will characterize innate immune sensors and downstream pathways that
are involved in the response to SARS-CoV-2 infection. These studies will inform the development of future
therapeutic interventions to prevent the morbidity and mortality of COVID-19 as well as other diseases
associated with inflammatory cytokine release.
