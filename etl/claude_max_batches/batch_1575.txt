title: Treatment and Mechanisms of Diabetic Fracture Healing
org_name: UNIVERSITY OF PENNSYLVANIA
phr: Project Narrative
Molecular mechanisms for diabetes impaired fracture healing are largely unknown. Ciliopathies
have very recently been identified as key modulators of the relationship between diabetes and
metabolic physiology. We will pursue this relationship to better understand diabetic fracture
healing and as a mechanistic tool for the engineering design of novel therapeutic aids for the
benefit of diabetic patients suffering from bone fractures.
