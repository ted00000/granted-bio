phr: PROJECT NARRATIVE The substance use crisis in the United States increasingly involves the co-use of methamphetamine and opioids together. Given the unique sociodemographic characteristics and co-morbidities associated with co- use, existing hospital-based addiction services may not adequately serve patients who use both substances. This project will quantify patient demographics and outcomes following hospital discharge, by substance use profile, and identify facilitators and barriers to expanding hospital-based addiction services tailored to co-use in the inpatient setting, through a sequential explanatory mixed methods design.
