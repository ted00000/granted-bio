application_id: 11310573
activity_code: K23
title: Targeting Neuropathogenesis of Altered Mental Status to Improve Survival in Cryptococcal Meningitis
org_name: UNIVERSITY OF MINNESOTA
phr: PROJECT NARRATIVE
 In HIV-associated cryptococcal meningitis, altered mental status is a common manifestation of
underlying neurological abnormalities and is associated with a significant increase in mortality. There is a
considerable lack of understanding as to the exact neurological abnormalities leading to the development of
altered mental status in cryptococcal meningitis. The objective of this study is to identify the underlying
neurological abnormalities in altered mental status during cryptococcal meningitis and to identify clinical
interventions that could reverse the neurological abnormalities and improve survival.
