title: Stem Cell Competition in Skin Homeostasis and Premalignancy
org_name: ROCKEFELLER UNIVERSITY
phr: Project Narrative
Cell competition homeostatically preserves and optimizes tissue function, but may also be hijacked by oncogenic
cells. However, it is unknown how “winner” cells sense and attack “loser” cells, nor how losers are eliminated by
winners. Using a premalignant model that switches between winner/oncogenesis and loser/homeostasis
depending on who their neighbors are, I propose to use this model to dissect stem cell competition during both
homeostasis and premalignancy.
