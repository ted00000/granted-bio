title: Ecological mHealth Cannabis Prevention for Youth Involved in the Juvenile Legal System
org_name: UNIVERSITY OF CALIFORNIA, SAN FRANCISCO
phr: PROJECT NARRATIVE
Youth involved in the legal system report elevate rates of cannabis use and are much more likely to develop
cannabis use disorder than non-involved peers. The proposed K23 project aims to use Ecological Momentary
Assessment to study cannabis use and Ecological Momentary Intervention to prevent cannabis use disorder
for youth involved in the legal system. The proposed research will expand the investigator’s capacities as an
independent health services researcher with expertise in: 1) cannabis use for youth involved in the legal
system, 2) secondary prevention of cannabis use disorder, 3) Ecological Momentary Assessment and
Intervention, 4) Personalized Feedback interventions, and 4) mobile health technology.
