Tuberculosis (TB) remains one of the leading infectious causes of death globally. Active case finding (ACF) can
help identify individuals with suspected TB and link them to care for earlier diagnosis, treatment, and prevention
of onward transmission. This proposal aims to optimize ACF strategies by pinpointing geographic locations with
