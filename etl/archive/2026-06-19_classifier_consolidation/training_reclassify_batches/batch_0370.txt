This proposed research describes a novel application of enantioselective photoredox catalysis to achieve stereochemical editing of quaternary stereocenters that is not established with current methods. Ultimately, the results of this proposed research will provide valuable tools to efficiently prepare small molecule drugs and their derivatives, and enhance the productivity of pharmaceutical research to advance human health.
