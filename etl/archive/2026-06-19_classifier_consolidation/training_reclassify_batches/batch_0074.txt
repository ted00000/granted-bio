title: Microbe dependent mechanisms that antagonize intestinal injury repair
org_name: CASE WESTERN RESERVE UNIVERSITY
phr: PROJECT NARRATIVE
Some patients with Crohn Disease have a yeast called Debaryomyces hansenii present within their ulcers, and
mouse models of intestinal injury show that this yeast impairs intestinal healing. The research described here
will help understand the mechanisms through which this yeast persists within the intestine and how it blocks
injury repair. This project will increase our understanding of how microbes that are associated with Crohn
Disease can affect disease course, with the goal of identifying new targets for future therapeutic development
to benefit Crohn Disease patients with D. hansenii infection.
