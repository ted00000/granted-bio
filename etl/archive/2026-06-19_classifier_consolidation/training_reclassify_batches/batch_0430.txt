Every step of development relies on the dynamic regulation of gene expression. The goal of my research is to
delineate mechanisms by which long non-coding RNAs (lncRNAs) recruit chromatin-modifying (i.e., epigenetic)
enzymes to regulate gene expression. The mechanisms established in this study will identify a paradigm that
will guide studies of other lncRNAs, RBPs, and silencing enzymes, which themselves are critical for embryonic
