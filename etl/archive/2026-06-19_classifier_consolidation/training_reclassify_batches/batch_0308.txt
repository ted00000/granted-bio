title: The contribution of lateral hypothalamus to cortical palatability coding and behavior
org_name: BRANDEIS UNIVERSITY
phr: Project Narrative The core pillars of this project are aimed at increasing our understanding of mammalian consumption behavior, the rejection of toxins, and the principles of sensory coding. As such, the work is directly related to improving human health in this era of obesity and binge-eating. Using electrophysiology in freely moving and consuming animals, along with pioneering computational techniques, I will examine the influence that the main feeding region, the lateral hypothalamus, has on cortical taste processing and behavior, paving the way for future research on intervention in disorders relating to hedonics and consumption.
