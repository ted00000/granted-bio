phr: Project Narrative Periodontal disease, a chronic inflammatory disease, affects about half of the US adult population and is linked to a number of serious systemic diseases. Neutrophils are immune cells that provide defense against pathogens, but they interact differently with some normally commensal (healthy) bacteria during disease, potentially allowing the bacteria to become harmful. This study will lead to a better understanding of pathogen-neutrophil relationships and will improve our ability to control periodontal disease and other chronic inflammatory diseases.
