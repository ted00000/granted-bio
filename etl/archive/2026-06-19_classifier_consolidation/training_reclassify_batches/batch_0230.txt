Educational Modules to Broaden Academic Research Cultures (EMBARC) is relevant to public health and the
mission of NIH/NIGMS because it innovatively leverages cultural frameworks to train and promote transfer of
underrepresented minority (URM) community college (CC) students to biobehavioral research career fields at
an early and critical period of professional and scientific development. We anticipate EMBARC trainees will be
equipped to succeed in biobehavioral research contexts and address the complex health needs of a rapidly
diversifying U.S. population. Our approach includes rigorous testing of culturally-informed educational activities
