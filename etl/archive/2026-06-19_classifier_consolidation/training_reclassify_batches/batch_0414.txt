title: Advancing the Approach to Subclinical Diastolic Dysfunction in Youth with Chronic Kidney Disease
org_name: LURIE CHILDREN'S HOSPITAL OF CHICAGO
phr: Project Narrative:
Young people with chronic kidney disease (CKD) have a high lifetime risk for cardiovascular disease, including
heart failure with preserved ejection fraction (HFpEF). However, youth with CKD have been largely excluded
from studies that improve detection of HFpEF at the earliest stages and from trials of medications that prevent
heart disease in adults with CKD. This study will leverage insights developed in adults with CKD to better
define early and subtle changes in heart function in youth with CKD and to begin to test the reversibility of
these changes using an interventional study.
