org_name: MASSACHUSETTS GENERAL HOSPITAL
phr: Project Narrative
Approximately half of patients with ALS and their care-partners experience emotional distress early after diagnosis, and
distress is linked with illness progression and dyadic functioning. Resilient Together-ALS (RT-ALS) is a dyadic, mind-
body meaning intervention to address emotional distress in ALS dyads early after diagnosis. RT-ALS has the potential to
address the gap in mind-body psychosocial care for dyads experiencing ALS with potential future adaptation in clinics
worldwide.
