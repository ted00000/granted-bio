application_id: 11138014
activity_code: K08
title: Osteoclast to Osteocyte Crosstalk in Accelerated Bone Turnover
org_name: YALE UNIVERSITY
phr: PROJECT NARRATIVE
This study focuses on how osteoclasts direct osteocytes to remove bone using lactation as a model. A better
understanding of how osteoclasts communicate with osteocytes to coordinate bone metabolism will expand our
knowledge of pathways that lead to excess bone loss. With the knowledge gained from this study, we ultimately
may be able to design new, more efficacious agents to target the abundant osteocyte for treatment of
osteoporosis.
