title: Multidisciplinary Virtual Care for Young Adults with Type 1 Diabetes Living in Rural Areas
org_name: UNIVERSITY OF KANSAS MEDICAL CENTER
phr: PROJECT NARRATIVE
Young adults with type 1 diabetes living in rural areas are a group particularly vulnerable to poor outcomes,
with higher hemoglobin A1c levels and lower use of diabetes technology such as continuous glucose monitors.
Current resources available to rural primary care providers fall short in providing the multidisciplinary care
required for optimal outcomes in this population. Therefore, building on Project ECHO-Diabetes, we will pilot
test the Rural Emerging/Young Adult CollaboraTive care – Type 1 Diabetes program to determine the
feasibility and acceptability of this intervention and provide preliminary data for a future fully-powered hybrid
randomized trial to test the implementation and effectiveness of the program to address existing inequities in
access to care.
