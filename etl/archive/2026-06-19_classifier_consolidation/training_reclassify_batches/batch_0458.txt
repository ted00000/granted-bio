Epidemiology in Kazakhstan (MoVE-Kaz). A partnership of key Kazakh institutions, supported by U.S. and
Belgian universities, 12 early career scientists and clinicians will be trained in molecular epidemiology and HIV
disease control and prevention.

Emerging and tropical infectious diseases are an important public health concern. Yet, there are
insufficient numbers of trained investigators to undertake related research, especially for vector-borne
diseases. This program will address this shortage by training future outstanding investigators in a
dynamic environment.
