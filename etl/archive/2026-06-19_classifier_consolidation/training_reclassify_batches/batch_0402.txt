title: Risk and resilience mechanisms underlying race disparities in ADRD: An examination of neighborhood resources, social networks, brain integrity, and cognition
org_name: UNIVERSITY OF MIAMI SCHOOL OF MEDICINE
phr: PROJECT NARRATIVE
Characterizing mechanisms that create and sustain racial disparities in Alzheimer's disease and
related dementias (ADRD) is of critical importance to the diverse, aging population of the U.S. The
proposed project will fill an important void in examining the extent to which neighborhood resources
contribute to disparities in brain and cognitive aging, as well as whether social networks can buffer
against neighborhood disadvantage. Results could have implications for interventions targeted at
multiple levels to prevent or delay age-related cognitive decline and reduce racial disparities in
ADRD.
