phr: RESEARCH NARRATIVE
Individuals with Down syndrome (DS, T21) have reduced cortical volume, fewer neurons and smaller
progenitor pools but how the extra copy of human chromosome 21 (HSA21) causes these features
remains unanswered. Proper expansion of neural progenitor cells (NPCs) is crucial to generating the

and/or early cell cycle exit contribute to reduced progenitor pools in T21. The project in this fellowship

and identify upstream molecular and cellular pathways to provide mechanistic insight into the impact of
T21 on neurogenesis.
