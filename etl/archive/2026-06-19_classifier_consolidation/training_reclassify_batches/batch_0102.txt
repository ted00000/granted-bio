title: Multi-omic Studies of Local and Systemic Immune Dysregulation in Rosacea
org_name: BRIGHAM AND WOMEN'S HOSPITAL
phr: Project Narrative The goals of our research are to 1) elucidate the mechanistic link between skin microbiota and local immune dysregulation in rosacea skin and 2) elucidate potential systemic immune dysregulation in rosacea patients. Unlike prior studies, we aim to employ multi-omics data integration to 1) explain the complex relationship of the microbial environment and the host immune response in the skin, and 2) characterize the systemic immune dysregulation that can explain the systemic disease burden in rosacea patients. We anticipate that our findings will lead to the development of novel preventative and therapeutic targets and shift the way we study and manage rosacea toward early detection and prevention of systemic co-morbidities.
