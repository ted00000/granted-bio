title: Validating a machine learning model of eye tracking in children with cortical visual impairment (CVI)
org_name: CHILDREN'S HOSPITAL OF LOS ANGELES
phr: Project narrative Cortical visual impairment (CVI) is the most common cause of pediatric visual impairment in developed countries, but there is no evidence-based treatment, in part due to the absence of a validated and comprehensive method of visual assessment that can be used as an outcome measure in clinical trials. The primary objective of this proposal is to use high resolution eye tracking and machine learning to develop and validate a CVI eye tracking severity score. The proposed studies will establish eye tracking as a quantitative, objective, and comprehensive measure of visual function in children with CVI, with widespread applicability in both clinical and research settings via the CVI eye tracking severity score, which will automate data interpretation.
