health care.

title: RiNGH (Research in Nursing & Global Health) Training Program
org_name: UNIVERSITY OF WASHINGTON
phr: PROJECT NARRATIVE
Comprising a significant proportion of the global health workforce, nurses are uniquely positioned to develop and
lead research to understand population health problems, inform health interventions, optimize health systems,
and promote health within communities and across populations. The Research in Nursing and Global Health
(RiNGH) training program aims to prepare junior nurse scientists with the knowledge and skills to advance global
health equity through team-based, interdisciplinary research. This training will provide enhanced education,
scholarly mentorship, and career development to pre-doctoral and post-doctoral trainees in nursing science.
