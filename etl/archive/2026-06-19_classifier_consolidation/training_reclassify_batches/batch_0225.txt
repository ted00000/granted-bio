title: A Novel Non-Drug, Non-Surgical Hydrogel-Based Treatment of Glaucoma
org_name: EMORY UNIVERSITY
phr: PROJECT NARRATIVE
Glaucoma is one of the leading causes of irreversible blindness worldwide, and current pharmacologic and
surgical treatment methods have low adherence rates and associated complications. The suprachoroidal
space can be expanded via a simple microneedle injection of a hydrogel and has been shown to reduce
intraocular pressure. In this proposal, I will determine the mechanism of intraocular pressure reduction from
suprachoroidal expansion, develop an optimal hydrogel for this application, and evaluate the safety and
