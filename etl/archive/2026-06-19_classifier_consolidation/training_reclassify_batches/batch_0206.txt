phr: PROJECT NARRATIVE
Children with rhabdomyosarcoma (RMS), the most common soft tissue sarcoma of childhood, suffer poor
outcomes due to high rates of recurrence. In this proposal, I will leverage experimental models of fusion-
negative to pinpoint the molecular control mechanisms governing intratumoral heterogeneity and cell state
identity in fusion-negative RMS. The results from this proposal will identify biomarkers of treatment efficacy,
and will build a foundation for future treatment options for RMS.
