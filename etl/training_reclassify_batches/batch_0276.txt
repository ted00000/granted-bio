expansion of a CGG short tandem repeat (STR) at the FMR1 gene on chromosome X where local DNA
methylation silences FMR1 expression. At the FMR1 locus and on multiple autosomes, we have recently found

expansion of the CGG STR at FMR1 spatially coordinates heterochromatinization and 3D genome miswiring.
