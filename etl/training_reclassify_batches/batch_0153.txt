phr: Project Narrative  Over the past two decades there has been a dramatic increase in the prevalence of inflammatory bowel disease, coincident with the obesity epidemic. One way to combat this problem is to understand more about how the expansion of fat tissue in the abdomen mediates inflammation of the gastrointestinal tract. The goal of this study is to understand how intra-abdominal fat tissue inflammation affects the gastrointestinal system and identify new approaches to alleviate inflammatory bowel disease.
