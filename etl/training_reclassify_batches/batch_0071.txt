title: Enhancer Hijacking of the IgH Locus as a Novel Genetic Mechanism of Inborn Errors of Immunity
org_name: WASHINGTON UNIVERSITY
phr: Project Narrative We are studying an inherited specific antibody deficiency (SAD), in which all affected individuals have a tandem duplication intersecting the immunoglobulin heavy chain (IgH) locus.
