title: Genome-driven identification of patient and bacterial drivers of resistance to novel beta-lactam/beta-lactamase inhibitor combination therapies
org_name: UNIVERSITY OF MICHIGAN AT ANN ARBOR
phr: Project Narrative
Carbapenem-resistant Klebsiella pneumoniae is an urgent public health threat with limited available treatments. Central to
maintaining long-term treatment efficacy is an improved understanding of the clinical practices, patient populations, and
bacterial factors that drive the emergence and spread of resistance. In this proposal, the PI will leverage isolate and data
collections from a regional healthcare network before and after the clinical deployment of two recently approved β-
lactam/β-lactamase inhibitor combination therapies to understand how baseline clinical practices and circulating bacteria
can inform the subsequent emergence and spread of resistance once an antibiotic is clinically deployed.
