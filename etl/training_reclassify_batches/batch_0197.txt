phr: Project Narrative In this project we seek to recruit and train minority high-school students who, in many cases, are the first in their family seeking to attend college. Our aim will be to facilitate this goal by giving them a broad array of STEM-related (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math) training and helping them construct an open- source dataset of labeled brain structures freely available to the public and learning community, then publish a scientific paper at the end of each summer detailing the results and distributing the data.
