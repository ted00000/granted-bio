title: Investigating Ensemble Activity Dynamics of a Thalamo-Striatal Circuit Regulating Alcohol Seeking
org_name: MEDICAL UNIVERSITY OF SOUTH CAROLINA
phr: PROJECT NARRATIVE
Alcohol use disorder is classically characterized by a loss of behavioral control, particularly in the face of negative
consequences, resulting in a loss of inhibitory control that may normally suppress alcohol seeking. I hypothesize
that a thalamo-striatal circuit, paraventricular thalamus projections to the nucleus accumbens, typically functions
to inhibit alcohol seeking but distinct ensembles within this circuit become dysregulated following dependence.
Using two-photon microscopy and single-cell optogenetics, I will identify distinct neuronal ensembles in this
critical circuit, determine dependence-induced dysregulation, and demonstrate the functional role of these
ensembles in regulating alcohol seeking.
