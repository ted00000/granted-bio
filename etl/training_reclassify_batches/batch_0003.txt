title: Improving Outcomes of Older Adults with Psychosocial Vulnerability Undergoing Major Surgery
org_name: UNIVERSITY OF TEXAS HLTH SCI CTR HOUSTON
phr: PROJECT NARRATIVE
The proposed research is relevant to public health because a growing number of older adults are undergoing
major surgery, which can lead to medical and functional decline. Thus, the proposed research seeks to
improve our knowledge of psychosocial vulnerability in older adults undergoing surgery to improve surgical
outcomes in this population. The project will determine the independent association of psychosocial
vulnerability with mortality and functional decline, explore how psychosocial vulnerability impacts post-
operative recovery, and pilot-test of a psychosocial intervention on post-operative functional recovery.
