title: Exosomal-mediated pyroptosis in ventilation-associated preterm brain injury
org_name: UNIVERSITY OF MIAMI SCHOOL OF MEDICINE
phr: PROJECT NARRATIVE Preterm infants who are mechanically ventilated face increased risk of developing brain injury and neurodevelopmental problems, but we do not understand the link between mechanical ventilation and increased risk of brain injury in preterm newborns. The goal of this proposal is to test the hypothesis that mechanical ventilation causes pulmonary cells to release vesicles that penetrate the brain and induce brain cell death. If we can understand what is causing brain cell death in mechanically ventilated preterm newborns, we may be able to develop therapeutic strategies to improve the neurodevelopmental outcomes of these vulnerable infants.
