title: Exploring mechanisms of activation of an innate immune pathway by fungal extracellular vesicles
org_name: MASSACHUSETTS GENERAL HOSPITAL
phr: PROJECT NARRATIVE
This proposal seeks to identify the mechanism by which a human fungal pathogen, like Candida
albicans, activates the cGAS-STING pathway. Our preliminary work has identified Ca DNA
packaged in extracellular vesicles (EVs) as the fungal ligand that triggers this pathway causing a
lethal inflammatory response that clears the fungal infection. This work proposed in this project
will uncover the functional consequence of this type I IFN activation by fungal pathogens,
elucidate the activation and regulation of this signaling, and uncover a new role for diverse fungal
EVs in host cell reprogramming.
