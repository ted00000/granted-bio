Mechanistic target of rapamycin (mTOR) is a highly conserved signaling network upregulated in a number of
proliferative lung diseases including pulmonary arterial hypertension (PAH) and lymphangioleiomyomatosis
(LAM). This proposal uses three transgenic mouse lines and primary PAH and LAM lung tissues to investigate
the role of mTOR hyperactivation on pulmonary vascular remodeling and pulmonary hypertension. The results

of vascular remodeling that can be harnessed to provide potential new therapeutic targets.
