title: Targeting the immunologic vulnerabilities of small cell lung carcinoma
org_name: UNIVERSITY OF MICHIGAN AT ANN ARBOR
phr: PROJECT NARRATIVE
Small cell lung cancer (SCLC) remains relatively refractory to multiple therapeutic modalities, including immune
checkpoint blockade, despite its high tumor mutation burden. This proposal seeks to exploit newfound
immunologic vulnerabilities of SCLC conferred by epigenetic control of MHC Class I antigen presentation.
Rational targeting of distinct immunologic subtypes of SCLC could lead to a deeper understanding of epigenetic
modulation of immunogenicity, and, ultimately, novel immunotherapies for this treatment-refractory tumor.
