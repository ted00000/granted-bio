title: Investigation of Chromatin Modifiers to Elucidate the Phenotypic Variability of Congenital Heart Disease in Patients with 22q11.2 Deletion Syndrome
org_name: ALBERT EINSTEIN COLLEGE OF MEDICINE
phr: PROJECT NARRATIVE: In 22q11.2 deletion syndrome, around 60% of patients present with cardiac defects, largely due to a deletion that includes TBX1; this provides a unique population in which to investigate possible genetic modifiers of heart development. Our lab performed whole genome sequence analysis on human subjects and discovered a subset of chromatin regulatory genes associated with heart defects in 22q11.2 deletion syndrome. This study aims to determine the mechanism by which Tbx1 interacts with these chromatin regulatory genes, particularly Kmt2c and Kmt2d, in mouse heart development.
