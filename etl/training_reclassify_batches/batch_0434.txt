title: Activity-Dependent Circuit Integration of Somatostatin Interneuron Subtypes in the Developing Neocortex
org_name: HARVARD MEDICAL SCHOOL
phr: Project Narrative
Recent work has uncovered incredible transcriptional diversity within the somatostatin cortical interneuron
class. Although much work has shown that interneuron circuit integration drives the proper maturation of
cortex, the precise efferent connectivity of cortical interneurons subtypes, and how this precision is achieved in
development, is unknown. This project seeks to link transcriptional diversity of somatostatin subtype specific
functional connectivity in cortical microcircuits, to examine how this specify arises in development and to
explore the role of developmental activity in this process.
