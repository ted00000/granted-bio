Suicide is the tenth leading cause of death in the U.S., second among individuals between the ages of 10 and
34 and fourth among individuals between the ages of 35 and 54. Rates of suicide have increased by over 30%
in the past two decades and continue to rise. The objective of this postdoctoral fellowship is to provide
scientists with the knowledge and skills to design and conduct research with the goal of reducing suicide-
related morbidity and mortality across the life course.
