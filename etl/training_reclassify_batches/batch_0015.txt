phr: Project Narrative
To fully understand how we hear, and develop effective treatments for hearing deficits, requires knowledge of
multiple disciplines, such as biology, medicine and engineering. This program trains junior scientists to be
experts in one discipline and familiar with several, so that they are able to focus the tools and techniques of
multiple disciplines on the problems to be solved.
