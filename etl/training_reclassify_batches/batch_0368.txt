This proposal will investigate how two subfamilies of cell surface proteins, the Dprs and DIPs, instruct specific connectivity in Drosophila central motor circuits. This study will also examine the signaling mechanisms underlying Dpr and DIP interactions, and potentially provide new signaling insights into their vertebrate orthologs, the IgLONs, which are associated with various neurological diseases.
