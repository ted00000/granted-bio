medication coating, and plastics – affect mood and cognition in middle and older age women. It will also drill

health, because while exposure is nearly ubiquitous, lifestyle changes can reduce such chemical exposures
and may alter disease trajectories.

title: The role of FGFR3 in the effects of PTH in the Mandibular Condyle
org_name: UNIVERSITY OF CONNECTICUT SCH OF MED/DNT
phr: Narrative
There is an urgent need for the development of non-invasive therapies for the degenerative process of the
temporomandibular joint. The proposed project addresses a novel hypothesis that intermittent administration of
parathyroid hormone (I-PTH) exerts a chondroprotective role, and the anabolic effects caused by its
administration would partially reverse degeneration of the TMJ of mice. Furthermore, we propose that these
effects are mainly regulated by FGFR3 signaling.

title: Clinical factors associated with long-term recovery following pediatric critical illness and injury
org_name: SEATTLE CHILDREN'S HOSPITAL
phr: PROJECT NARRATIVE
Traumatic injury contributes to more deaths among children and adolescents in the United States than all other
causes combined, and is the leading cause of preventable morbidity in pediatrics with over 100,000 children a
year at risk of experiencing long-term impairments after severe injury. The goal of this proposal is to identify
potentially modifiable post-injury clinical factors that contribute to poor long-term outcomes after pediatric
trauma. Improving clinical management after an injury has occurred in order to optimize recovery may
complement injury prevention efforts to reduce the overall burden of severe traumatic injury in children.
