phr: Project Narrative
Women and infants face substantial risks of illness and death during pregnancy and after birth. Group
antenatal care is an innovative model that has effectively addressed many of these mental and physical health
challenges, both in the US and globally. The purpose of this study is to understand how community building, or
how the experience of being in a group intervention during pregnancy, contributes to mental and physical
outcomes.
