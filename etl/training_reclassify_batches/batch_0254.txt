title: Quantification of Iron Oxide Nanoparticles in Cryopreserved Kidneys with Longitudinally Detected Electron Paramagnetic Resonance
org_name: UNIVERSITY OF MINNESOTA
phr: Project Narrative The proposed research is aimed at engineering a novel longitudinally-detected electron paramagnetic resonance system for inexpensively and noninvasively quantifying iron oxide nanoparticles in tissues. This system will be used to determine concentrations of nanoparticles in rat kidneys that have been cryopreserved for transplantation. The measurements from this system will be used to address bottlenecks in kidney cryopreservation research, thus increasing the number and quality of kidneys available for transplant.
