science biologic measures including mother's own milk (MOM) volume, secretory activation, and biomarkers
(sodium, citrate, lactose, protein) in pump-dependent mothers of preterm and critically ill infants. Results of this
prospective cohort study targeting the biology and physiology of insufficient MOM among NICU mothers are
expected to move science towards filling the gap in understanding these mechanisms with gestational age as a
